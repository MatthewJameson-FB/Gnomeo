What happens after approval

  • We create your workspace manually.
  • We generate a private portal link.
  • You upload Google Ads and Meta Ads CSVs in the portal.
  • Reports, memory, and history build up over time.

What this is not

  • No fake checkout.
  • No public customer data.
  • No full login/auth yet.
  • No automated billing yet.

Privacy posture

Request details are used only for onboarding review and workspace setup. Raw ad CSVs are handled later in the private portal, not on this page.

Who it suits

Analyst-style teams that want a conservative review of messy ad exports without a bloated SaaS signup flow.
