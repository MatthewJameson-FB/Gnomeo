Example report

Ecommerce skincare account audit

Sample data only. This example shows the shape of a conservative report for a UK skincare ecommerce account.

Sample dataUK ecommerceROAS 1.19xWasted spend £14k

Sample account context

  • UK skincare ecommerce brand
  • Monthly ad spend: £57,300.00
  • Current ROAS: 1.19x
  • Estimated wasted spend: £14,325.00

What the report found

  • A clear reallocation from Meta Prospecting Broad UK into Google Brand Search.
  • A monitor call on the weaker prospecting campaign.
  • A controlled test on Google Brand Search instead of an aggressive scale.

Recommendation snippets

Sample data only
Reduce £2,400.00 from Meta Prospecting Broad UK → increase Google Brand Search by £2,400.00.

Rationale: the waste is concentrated enough to justify a shift, but not big enough to remove the audit layer.

Rationale snippets

  • Spend is meaningful and performance is weaker than the account average.
  • The strongest move is a controlled reallocation, not a bigger total budget.
  • Monitor/test items stay in place when the export is too thin to trust safely.

Why conservative notes matter

This example is intentionally cautious. It shows how Gnomeo can point to real waste and still keep the move controlled when the export quality is mixed.
