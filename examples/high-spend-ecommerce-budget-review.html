Example report

High-spend ecommerce budget review

Sample data only. This example is built around a higher-spend ecommerce account where the signal is a little cleaner and the report can be more direct.

Sample dataHigh spendBudget reviewControlled reallocation

Sample account context

  • High-spend ecommerce account
  • Monthly spend: £61,790.00
  • Current ROAS: 3.72x
  • Estimated waste: £15,447.50

What the report found

  • A clear move from Meta Prospecting - Broad into Meta Retargeting - Cart/ViewContent.
  • A controlled increase on the stronger retargeting campaign.
  • A monitoring note where the audit says the move should stay gradual.

Recommendation snippets

Sample data only
Reduce £1,854.00 from Meta Prospecting - Broad → increase Meta Retargeting - Cart/ViewContent by £1,854.00.

Rationale: the stronger signal sits in retargeting, so the report prefers a measured reallocation.

Confidence and rationale

  • The report reads more like analyst commentary than a scorecard.
  • Confidence stays moderate because the signal is actionable but still audited.
  • Conservative notes are short and only appear when they add information.

Why conservative notes matter

This example shows the balance the product is aiming for: useful enough to guide a budget conversation, but cautious enough to keep trust intact.
