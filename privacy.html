Privacy and temporary processing

Gnomeo keeps the handling simple: process the export, generate the report, and remove free-tier uploads after processing.

What Gnomeo processes

  • Uploaded Google Ads and Meta Ads campaign exports.
  • Basic submission details needed to return the report.
  • Operational metadata such as timestamp, file size, platform inference, and report status.

What we do not do

  • No automated campaign changes.
  • No media buying or account management.
  • No selling of uploaded data.

Temporary processing

  • Free-tier uploads are processed temporarily to generate the report and are automatically deleted after processing.
  • Temporary files are treated as disposable processing material, not long-term storage.
  • We keep only the minimum operational record needed to show that a request was handled.

Retention and cleanup

  • Do not include unnecessary personal data in the export.
  • Uploaded CSVs and temporary processing copies are deleted after processing.
  • Generated reports are kept only as long as needed to preview or deliver the result.
