Data handling

How uploads are handled

Temporary processing, conservative analysis, and a narrow retention model are the default.

How uploads are processed

  • Campaign exports are checked, analysed, and used to generate a report.
  • Processing is conservative: Gnomeo recommends cut, scale, hold, or test only when the signal supports it.
  • Reports are built from the export you provide, not from hidden account access.

Temporary file handling

  • Uploaded files are treated as temporary processing inputs.
  • Free-tier uploads are automatically cleaned up after the report process finishes.
  • Partial failures and exceptions still trigger cleanup of temporary input files.

Secure transport

  • Traffic should be served over HTTPS in production.
  • Do not upload sensitive data you do not need for analysis.
  • Temporary processing is meant to keep exposure low, not to promise perfect secrecy.

Minimal retention

  • Keep only lightweight operational metadata where it is genuinely useful.
  • Do not retain raw export rows longer than needed.
  • Do not retain generated reports unless there is a clear operational reason.

Operating posture

Gnomeo stays independent, conservative, and signal-aware. It does not manage campaigns, buy media, or make claims it cannot support.
